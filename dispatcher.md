For vendors without smartphone access, we can plan to have agents who work between the recommender system and the vendors.

Periodically, maybe every Friday, the system runs a batch process to aggregate the top-performing search queries for items categorized under the vendors' specialities. This data is compiled into a weekly report accessible to the agent who has digital access to the system.

The agent can then print this report and share with vendors in their categories of products. With this report, vendors are informed of specific items in demand, allowing them to adjust production or prioritize inventory for the coming week.
